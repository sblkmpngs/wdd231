Newcastle Tour Guide

Site Discription

The Newcastle tour guide is a website tour guide that is going re-introduce Newcastle to the people. This website is going to contain some of Newcastle's greatest attractions, and will urge you to book a tour today.

Site Purpose

The reason I decided to do this website is because I feel that because Newcastle is a small town, people do not give themselves a chance to find out more about it, and that can be said for even the locals. This website will provide people a chance to get to know the town even more.
